EVENT 06

Hovercraft

Manual — RC
About the Event

Pilot a custom-built RC hovercraft through a challenging course. Your hovercraft must be self-built within specified dimensions and weight limits. The course includes turns, ramps, and surface changes. Master the unique air-cushion physics — speed and precision wins. One-on-one battles decide who advances.

2–4Team Size
✈️ Night Light1st Prize
MQ-1352nd Prize
Rules & Regulations
  • 🛸 Hovercraft must be self-built; no commercial hovercrafts allowed.
  • 📐 Maximum dimensions: 40cm × 40cm × 30cm (L × W × H).
  • ⚖️ Maximum weight: 4 kg including battery.
  • 🔋 Must run on batteries only — no wired power during competition.
  • 🎮 RC controller must operate on standard 2.4 GHz frequency.
  • 🏆 Hovercraft should be hover during the whole match, Maneuverability is the main criteria.
  • ⚔️ Matches are 1v1; the hovercraft that stays in bounds or scores more points wins.
  • ⏱️ Each match slot is 20 minutes long.
  • ⏱️ The matches will be of 2 minutes for each team.
  • 🚫 Intentional interference with opponent's controller is strictly prohibited.
  • 📋 Teams must report to the arena at least 5 minutes before their slot.
  • 🏆 League format: 5 match slots, top 2 teams advance to grand final.
  • 🏆 Judgement criteria: 50% Control, 25% maneuverability, 25% Aggression
  • 🏆 Judges desicion will be Final.
